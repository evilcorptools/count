Last Updated: September 4, 2025

This policy outlines the procedures for order cancellations and refunds for the RA1 hardware product.

Order Cancellation

You may cancel your order for a full refund at any time before the order has been shipped. To cancel an order, please contact us immediately through our contact page with your order number.

If the order has already been shipped, it cannot be canceled. In this case, you may follow the return process outlined below.
